ChatGPT is a free AI tool that can help you earn $50–$200/month by writing blog posts, social media content, or product descriptions for freelance clients. Here’s how to start:

  • Sign Up: Create a free account at chat.openai.com.
  • Learn Prompts: Use prompts like “Write a 500-word blog post on fitness tips” to generate content fast.
  • Find Gigs: Join platforms like Upwork or Fiverr, offering AI-written content for $10–$50 per job.
  • Upgrade for More: Get ChatGPT Plus for $20/month to access advanced features (optional, try Grammarly Premium for AI writing polish).
